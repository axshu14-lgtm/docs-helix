Helixia has a built-in system for members to submit suggestions and feedback. Your input helps shape the server's future features and improvements.

## How to Submit

The suggestion panel in the [panel](https://discord.com/channels/1459854752580898868/1459854756607561901) channel. It has two buttons:

### Suggestion Button

Click **Suggestion** to open a form where you can type your idea. You have up to **4,000 characters** to describe your suggestion in detail.

After submitting:

- Your suggestion is posted with upvote and downvote buttons so the community can weigh in
- Staff can accept or reject suggestions
- You and others can add follow-up comments

### Feedback Button

Click **Feedback** to open a form for general feedback. This is for thoughts that aren't specific feature suggestions — maybe something about the server experience, things you'd like to see improved, or general comments.

Feedback also allows up to **4,000 characters**.

## Guidelines

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What makes a good suggestion">
    - Be specific about what you want and why
    - Explain the problem your suggestion solves
    - Consider how it would work from a user's perspective
    - Check if someone has already suggested something similar
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What to avoid">
    - Vague requests like "make the bot better"
    - Duplicate suggestions that have already been submitted
    - Suggestions that are technically impossible or impractical
    - Troll or joke submissions
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Voting

After a suggestion  is posted, anyone can vote on it using the upvote and downvote buttons in the [suggestions](https://discord.com/channels/1459854752580898868/1459854756607561902) channel. This helps staff understand which suggestions the community cares about most.

<Note>
  Members who abuse the suggestion system may be temporarily banned from submitting suggestions. This doesn't affect your ability to use the rest of the bot.
</Note>

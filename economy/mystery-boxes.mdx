Mystery boxes are containers that hold a random amount of Pokécoins. Each box, when opened, gives you between **30,000 and 150,000 PC**.

## How to Open Boxes

Use the `.openbox` command (or `.ob` for short):

```text
.openbox 1        — open 1 box
.openbox 5        — open 5 boxes at once
.openbox all      — open all your boxes
```

When you open boxes, the bot will show you what each box contained and your updated balance.

## How to Earn Boxes

There are several ways to earn mystery boxes. They accumulate automatically based on your activity.

### Messages

For every **100 messages** you send in the [chat](https://discord.com/channels/1459854752580898868/1459854755378499792) channel, you earn **1 mystery box**. Only messages in the tracked channel count — messages in other channels don't contribute to this.

<Note>
  This is the same channel that counts toward your "Send 100 Messages" daily quest. Two benefits from one activity.
</Note>

### Vanity Status

If your custom Discord status starts with `.gg/helixia` or `discord.gg/helixia`, the bot tracks your vanity time. For every **24 hours** of continuous vanity time, you earn **1 mystery box**.

There are requirements to be eligible — see the [Vanity guide](/community/vanity) for full details.

### Server Tag

Keeping the server tag active earns you **1 mystery box every 72 hours**.

There are requirements to be eligible — see the [Server tag guide](/community/server-tag) for full details.

### Giveaways

Some giveaways award mystery boxes as prizes. The number of boxes depends on the specific giveaway.

## Box Rewards

Each mystery box gives a random reward:

| Minimum   | Maximum    | Rounding                 |
| --------- | ---------- | ------------------------ |
| 30,000 PC | 150,000 PC | Rounded to nearest 1,000 |

The reward is determined randomly when you open the box. Every box has an equal chance of landing anywhere in that range.

<Tip>
  Opening boxes in bulk (`.openbox all`) is the fastest way. There's no advantage to opening them one at a time — the rewards are random regardless.
</Tip>

## Checking Your Boxes

Run `.info` to see how many unopened boxes you have. The **Boxes** field shows your current count.

You can also run `.helpbox` for an in-bot explanation of the box system.

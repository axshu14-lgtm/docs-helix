Helixia rewards active chatters. Every **100 messages** you send in the [chat](https://discord.com/channels/1459854752580898868/1459854755378499792) channel earns you **1 mystery box**, which contains anywhere between **30,000 and 150,000 PC**. It's one of the most passive and consistent ways to stack up boxes just by being part of the community.

---

## How It Works

The bot tracks every message you send in [chat](https://discord.com/channels/1459854752580898868/1459854755378499792). Once your count hits a multiple of 100, a mystery box is automatically added to your inventory. You don't need to run any command or claim anything — the box just appears.

| Activity                                                                                           | Reward              |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Every 100 messages in [chat](https://discord.com/channels/1459854752580898868/1459854755378499792) | 1 Mystery Box       |
| Each Mystery Box                                                                                   | 30,000 – 150,000 PC |

<Note>
  These are the same messages that count toward your **"Send 100 Messages"** daily quest. One activity, two rewards — quest credit and a box.
</Note>

---

## What Counts as a Valid Message

Not every message you send is counted. The bot applies the following rules automatically:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Must be sent in chat">
    Messages sent in any other channel — including bot commands channels, gambling channels, or anywhere else — do not count toward your message total. Only 🌏〢world-chat is tracked.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Must be at least 3 characters long">
    Messages shorter than 3 characters are ignored. Single-letter responses, emoji-only messages shorter than the threshold, and similar low-effort messages will not be counted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Must not be sent within 2 seconds of your last message">
    If you send a message less than 2 seconds after your previous one, it is not counted. This prevents rapid-fire spam from inflating your message count. Each counted message must have at least a 2-second gap from the one before it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Must not be your 4th or more consecutive message in a row">
    If you send 3 messages in a row without anyone else sending a message in between, any further consecutive messages from you will not be counted until someone else chats. This prevents one person from stacking their count by talking to themselves.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Warning>
  All four of these rules are enforced automatically by the bot. There is no way to appeal a message not being counted — if it didn't meet the criteria above, it simply doesn't count.
</Warning>

## Checking Your Progress

Run `.info` to see your current message count and how many unopened boxes you have waiting:

| Field        | What It Shows                                                    |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Messages** | Your total counted messages in tracked channels since you joined |
| **Boxes**    | The number of mystery boxes currently in your inventory          |

You can also run `.i` as a shorthand for the same command.

---

## Opening Your Boxes

Once you've earned boxes, open them with `.openbox`:

```text
.openbox 1      — open 1 box
.openbox 5      — open 5 boxes at once
.openbox all    — open everything at once
```

Each box gives a random amount between **30,000 and 150,000 PC**, rounded to the nearest 1,000. Run `.helpbox` for an in-bot breakdown of the box system.

<Tip>
  There is no benefit to opening boxes one at a time. Use `.openbox all` to open everything in one go — the rewards are random regardless of how many you open at once.
</Tip>

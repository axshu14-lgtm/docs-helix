**Pokécoins (PC)** are the primary currency in the Helixia server. You earn them through daily activities, open them from mystery boxes, win them in events, and can even claim them through the Pokétwo marketplace.

## How to Check Your Balance

Run `.info` (or `.bal`) to see your current profile. The **Balance** field shows your available Pokécoins.

Your profile also shows:

- **Messages** — total messages you've sent in the [chat](https://discord.com/channels/1459854752580898868/1459854755378499792) channels
- **Boxes** — unopened [mystery boxes](/economy/mystery-boxes) waiting to be opened
- **Total Claimed** — lifetime Pokécoins you've claimed through the claim panel
- **Vanity Duration** — total time your vanity status has been active
- **Tag Duration** — total time your server tag has been active
- **Current Level** — your highest level role

## Ways to Earn Pokécoins

There are many ways to earn Pokécoins. Here's a breakdown of every source, roughly ordered by how much you'll earn from each.

### Daily Quests

The single best way to earn Pokécoins consistently. Complete all three quests plus the bonus every day.

| Source                       | Reward     |
| ---------------------------- | ---------- |
| Send 100 Messages            | 100,000 PC |
| Participate in 1 Daily Event | 30,000 PC  |
| Place 5 Auction Bids         | 25,000 PC  |
| **Bonus** (complete all 3)   | 150,000 PC |
| **Weekend Bonus** (Sat/Sun)  | 200,000 PC |

That's up to **355,000 PC per day** on weekdays and **405,000 PC on weekends** — just from quests alone.

On top of that, quest streaks add an extra **10,000 to 70,000 PC per day** depending on your current streak day.

### Mystery Boxes

Mystery boxes contain a random amount of Pokécoins between **30,000 and 150,000 PC** each. You earn boxes through:

| How                  | Rate                   |
| -------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Sending messages     | 1 box per 100 messages |
| Vanity status active | 1 box per 24 hours     |
| Server tag active    | 1 box per 72 hours     |
| Giveaway prizes      | Varies per giveaway    |

## Understanding Your Profile

When you run `.info`, you'll see several fields. Here's what each one means:

| Field               | What It Means                                                                                                                                                                     |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Messages**        | The total number of messages you've sent in whitelisted (tracked) channels since you joined. Not all channels count — only the ones specifically designated for message tracking. |
| **Boxes**           | The number of unopened mystery boxes you currently have. Open them with `.openbox`.                                                                                               |
| **Balance**         | Your current spendable Pokécoins. This is what you can use to gamble, transfer, or claim.                                                                                         |
| **Total Claimed**   | The lifetime total of Pokécoins you've claimed through the claim panel. This number only goes up.                                                                                 |
| **Vanity Duration** | How long your custom Discord status has contained the server's vanity link. Shown in days, hours, and minutes.                                                                    |
| **Tag Duration**    | How long you've had the server tag active. Shown in days, hours, and minutes.                                                                                                     |
| **Current Level**   | Your highest level role, determined by the level roles configured in the server.                                                                                                  |

<Tip>
  Your `.info` also has interactive buttons at the bottom — **Daily Quests**, **Achievements**, **Transfers**, and **Gambling** — that let you quickly jump to those sections without running additional commands.
</Tip>

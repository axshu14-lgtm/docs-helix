The auction system keeps detailed records of every sale, purchase, and bid that happens in Helixia. You can view your personal auction history, check server-wide all-time stats, and compete on six different leaderboards — all through public commands available to every member.

---

## Your Auction Profile

Run `/profile` (or `,p`) to view your personal auction stats at any time.

Your profile shows:

| Stat | What It Tracks |
|---|---|
| **Bids Placed** | Total number of valid bids you have placed across all sessions |
| **Auctions Won** | Total number of Pokémon you have successfully purchased |
| **Total Spent** | Cumulative Pokécoins you have paid across all winning bids |
| **Pokémon Sold** | Total number of your Pokémon that have been successfully auctioned and traded |
| **Total Earned** | Cumulative Pokécoins you have earned from all completed sales |
| **Bids on Your Listings** | Total number of bids other members have placed on Pokémon you listed |

<Tip>
  You can view another member's auction profile by mentioning them or providing their user ID: `/profile @user` or `,p @user`. This is useful for checking whether a seller has a strong track record before a session.
</Tip>

## Server-Wide Auction Statistics

Run `/aucstats` to see all-time aggregate statistics for the entire Helixia auction system.

The stats embed shows:

| Stat | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Unique Sellers** | Total number of distinct members who have ever sold a Pokémon |
| **Total Auctions** | Combined count of all sold and unsold slots across all sessions |
| **Pokémon Sold** | Number of slots that ended with a successful sale |
| **Pokémon Unsold** | Number of slots where no valid bids were received |
| **Total Pokécoins Earned** | The combined value of every completed sale, ever |
| **Volume (Millions)** | Total PC volume expressed in millions for easy reading |
| **Highest Bid** | The single largest winning bid in Helixia auction history |
| **Total Bids Placed** | Combined number of all bids ever placed across every session |
| **Highest Bid Details** | The Pokémon name and buyer behind the all-time highest sale |

These numbers update live after every auction event. On a busy day with ~150 Pokémon sold, you can watch the volume climb in real time.

## Auction Leaderboards

Run `/auclb` to open the interactive auction leaderboard. There are **six leaderboard categories** to explore, selectable from a dropdown menu:

| Category | What It Ranks |
|---|---|
| **Most Pokémon Sold** | Sellers with the highest number of completed sales (ties broken by total earned) |
| **Most Pokémon Bought** | Buyers with the highest number of successful purchases |
| **Highest Total Spent** | Members who have spent the most Pokécoins across all purchases |
| **Highest Total Earned** | Members who have earned the most Pokécoins from selling |
| **Most Bids Placed** | Members with the highest total bidding activity (bids placed, not wins) |
| **Biggest Purchases** | The largest individual sale amounts ever recorded, including the Pokémon name, buyer, and seller |

### Navigating the Leaderboard

- Use the **dropdown menu** to switch between leaderboard categories
- Use the **Prev** and **Next** buttons to page through results (10 entries per page, up to 25 shown)
- Your **personal rank** in the currently selected category is shown in the footer of the embed, so you always know where you stand even if you're not in the top 10
- The leaderboard is **live** — it updates automatically after each auction event, no need to refresh manually

<Note>
  The leaderboard is interactive for **10 minutes** after you open it. After that, the buttons and dropdown become inactive. Simply run `/auclb` again to open a fresh session.
</Note>

## Auction Timings

Run `/auctimings` to see the scheduled start times for all three daily auction sessions, displayed in **your local timezone**.

The output shows each slot with a dynamic Discord timestamp:

| Slot | Scheduled Time (IST) |
|---|---|
| Slot 1 | 6:00 PM |
| Slot 2 | 9:00 PM |
| Slot 3 — Final | 11:30 PM |

Because Discord timestamps automatically convert to your local time, you don't need to manually convert from IST. The embed will also show a relative countdown (e.g., "in 2 hours") so you know exactly how long until the next session starts.

<Tip>
  Run `/auctimings` when you're not sure whether an auction is happening soon. It's the quickest way to check the schedule without asking in chat.
</Tip>

## How Stats Feed Into Achievements

Your auction activity doesn't only appear in your profile — it also counts toward two **permanent achievement categories** that pay out large one-time Pokécoin rewards:

| Achievement Category | What It Tracks | Max Reward |
|---|---|---|
| **Pokémon Sold** | Total Pokémon you've sold through auctions | 2,000,000 PC |
| **Auctions Won** | Total Pokémon you've bought through auctions | 2,000,000 PC |

Both categories have four milestone tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond) at 10, 25, 50, and 75 completions each.

## Where Stats Are Tracked

All auction stats are tracked against your account in the **main Helixia server**, regardless of whether the activity happened in the temporary auction server. You never need to worry about which server you're in — everything syncs to your main profile automatically.

Stats are updated in real time:

- **Bids Placed** — updated the moment a valid bid is accepted
- **Auctions Won / Total Spent** — updated when the trade thread is completed and verified
- **Pokémon Sold / Total Earned** — updated when the trade thread is completed and verified

This means stats only reflect **fully completed, verified trades**. Winning a bid but failing to complete the trade does not count toward your Auctions Won or Total Spent.

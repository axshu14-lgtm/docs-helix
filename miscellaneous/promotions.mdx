Helixia does not conduct promotions or sponsorships of any kind. This page exists to make that position clear so there is no ambiguity around it.

---

## Our Stance

Helixia does not promote, advertise, sponsor, or partner with any external Discord servers, bots, communities, or products — whether paid or free.

This is a deliberate choice, not an oversight. We have no interest in turning Helixia into an advertising platform for outside communities or services. Our focus is entirely on providing the best possible experience for our own members, and we believe that goal is best served by keeping Helixia free of promotional noise.

---

## What This Means

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="We do not accept paid promotions">
    No amount of payment will result in Helixia promoting another server, bot, or product to our members. We do not sell promotional slots, shoutout packages, advertisement placements, or anything of that nature.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="We do not accept free promotions or partnership requests">
    We also do not conduct free mutual promotions, partnership exchanges, or collab arrangements with other Discord servers or bots. Requests of this nature are declined regardless of the size or reputation of the server making the request.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="We do not promote other bots">
    Helixia will not advertise, recommend, or sponsor third-party Discord bots on behalf of their developers, whether as a paid deal or a free arrangement.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Unsolicited promotion requests are ignored">
    If you DM staff or send a message in the server requesting a promotion, partnership, or sponsorship deal, it will not receive a response. Please do not reach out for this.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

---

## Why

We simply do not like it. Promotions and partnerships introduce conflicts of interest, clutter the server experience, and shift the focus away from what actually matters — the community itself.

Helixia has no plans to change this position.

---

<Note>
  This policy applies to all forms of external promotion — paid, free, mutual, or otherwise. No exceptions are made regardless of who is asking or what is being offered.
</Note>

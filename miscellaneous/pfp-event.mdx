The PFP Event is Helixia's profile picture contest, where members compete to see who has the best profile picture. Entries are submitted, reviewed by staff, and then put to a community vote. The members with the most votes at the end of the voting period win massive Pokécoin prizes.

The event is run entirely by **Mogger**, Helixia's dedicated event bot.

---

## Event Format

- **Type:** Solo — each participant enters individually with their own profile picture
- **Structure:** Single round — there are no elimination brackets or multiple stages. All approved entries compete simultaneously in one voting phase
- **Registration period:** 5 days from the announcement date
- **Voting period:** 2 weeks after registration closes

<Note>
  The first Helixia PFP Event featured **120 approved entries** and **4,927 total votes** cast across the entire event — making it one of the most participated community events in the server's history. Duo formats may be explored in future editions.
</Note>

## How to Enter

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set your desired profile picture">
    Before registering, make sure the profile picture you want to compete with is already set on your Discord account. This is the picture that will represent you in the contest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Participate button">
    Head to the PFP Event registration channel and click the **Participate** button on the Mogger bot's event message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your DMs">
    Mogger will DM you immediately after you click the button. You have **3 minutes** to respond — keep your DMs open before clicking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your profile picture">
    Follow the instructions in the DM and send your profile picture within the 3-minute window.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for staff review">
    Your submission goes to staff for review before it is approved. This step exists to prevent inappropriate, misleading, or invalid entries from entering the contest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your entry goes live">
    Once approved, your profile picture will appear in the voting channel. Members can then vote for it during the voting phase.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Do **not** submit any of the following — they will be rejected immediately:
  - Gore, NSFW, or sexually explicit images
  - Political imagery
  - Offensive, hateful, or inappropriate content
  - Someone else's artwork or image submitted without context

  Submitting disqualifying content may result in further action beyond just rejection.
</Warning>

## Entry Requirements

To be eligible to participate as an entrant:

- Your Discord account must be at least **1 month old**
- You may submit **one entry only** — no resubmissions or swaps after approval
- Your submitted image must be appropriate and pass staff review

---

## How Voting Works

Once registration closes, the voting channel opens and all approved entries are posted there by Mogger. Each entry has two buttons:

- **Vote** — cast your vote for that entry
- **Voters** — see who has voted for that entry

You can scroll through all entries and vote for whichever profile picture you think deserves to win.

### Voting Requirements

To have your vote count as valid, you must meet **all** of the following:

- Your Discord account must be at least **1 month old**
- You must have at least **2 self-assignable roles** assigned from the self-roles channel
- You must **remain in Helixia** for the duration of the event — leaving the server after voting will invalidate your votes
- You **cannot vote for your own submission**
- You **can** vote for other entries even if you are a participant yourself

<Warning>
  Votes from accounts that do not meet the above requirements are automatically disqualified and do not count toward any entry's total. Make sure you meet all criteria before voting.
</Warning>

## Rules

### General Rules

- **J2J / J4J voting is allowed** — asking friends to join Helixia to vote for you, or voting for each other, is permitted
- **No begging, spamming, or pressuring members for votes inside Helixia** — you may promote yourself externally, but not within the server
- **Server promotions and giveaways in other servers are allowed** — you are free to promote your entry outside of Helixia
- **Staff members may participate** under the same rules as regular members — no special treatment

### Disqualification

The following will result in your entry being removed and your disqualification from the event:

- Any form of **unfair vote manipulation** — coordinated mass-joining of accounts for votes, fake accounts, botted votes, or any other artificial inflation of your vote count
- Breaking any of the rules listed above
- Submitting an inappropriate profile picture

<Warning>
  Breaking rules may also result in your existing votes being removed before disqualification is applied. Staff have full visibility into vote logs and activity patterns. Vote manipulation is taken seriously.
</Warning>

### Staff Conduct

Staff members are not permitted to have any kind of improper interactions with the voting process. Staff who participate do so as regular members and have no access to voting data or the ability to influence results in their favour.

## Leaderboard

During the voting phase, Mogger posts and periodically updates a live **PFP Event Leaderboard** in the voting channel showing the current top 10 entries by vote count, along with each entry's username and total votes.

The leaderboard also displays:
- Total number of participants
- Total votes cast so far
- When the voting phase ends

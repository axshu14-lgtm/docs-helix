The leveling system in Helixia rewards you for being an active member of the community. As you gain experience and reach new level milestones, you receive **one-time Pokécoin rewards** automatically added to your balance — the higher the level, the bigger the payout.

## How Leveling Works

Leveling in Helixia is powered by the **Arcane bot**. Arcane tracks your activity and experience, and when you hit a new level threshold it assigns you the corresponding level role in the server. The Helixia bot watches for these role assignments — the moment it detects you've received a new level role, it automatically credits your Pokécoin reward.

This means the flow works like this:

<Steps>
  <Step title="You gain XP">
    Chat and stay active in the server. Arcane tracks your experience in the background.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arcane assigns your level role">
    When you reach a milestone level, Arcane automatically gives you the corresponding level role and announces your level-up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Helixia detects the new role">
    The Helixia bot sees that you've received a new level role and triggers the reward for that level.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your Pokécoins are credited">
    The reward is instantly added to your Helixia balance. You'll receive a notification in the server confirming the amount you earned.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Because Helixia depends on Arcane assigning the level role, the two bots must both be online and functioning for rewards to be processed correctly. If Arcane is experiencing issues, level-up rewards may be delayed.
</Note>

## Key Details

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Rewards are one-time only">
    Each level reward can only be earned once per milestone. If you've already received the reward for a particular level, it will not be awarded again — even if you somehow lose and regain the role.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Your level is shown on your profile">
    Run `.info` to view your profile. The **Current Level** field displays the highest level role you currently hold in the server, as determined by Arcane.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Only milestone levels give rewards">
    Not every level has a reward. Rewards are tied to specific milestone levels (5, 10, 15, 20, 30, 40, 50, 65, 80, 100). Levels in between — like Level 7 or Level 25 — do not have associated Pokécoin payouts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Higher levels take longer to reach">
    Experience requirements increase as you level up. Lower levels like 5 and 10 are relatively quick to reach through normal activity, while higher milestones like Level 80 and Level 100 require sustained long-term activity.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting — Missing Level-Up Reward

If you leveled up but did **not** receive your Pokécoin reward, here's what's likely happened and what to do about it.

### Why This Can Happen

Because the Helixia bot relies on Arcane assigning your level role to trigger the reward, if Arcane **did not assign the role** (or assigned it with a delay), Helixia never gets the signal to pay out the reward. This is an Arcane-side issue and is outside of Helixia's control.

Common scenarios where this occurs:

- **Arcane was temporarily offline or restarting** when you leveled up, so the role was never assigned
- **The role assignment was delayed** and Helixia missed the event window
- **A sync issue between the two bots** caused the role update to go undetected

<Warning>
  If you leveled up and did not receive your reward, **do not ignore it**. Open a support ticket so staff can manually verify your level and issue the missing reward.
</Warning>

### What to Do

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm the level-up happened">
    Check whether you actually have the new level role in the server. You can verify this by running `.info` and checking your **Current Level**, or by looking at your roles in the server member list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your balance">
    Run `.info` to check your current Pokécoin balance. If the reward was credited, you'll see the change reflected there.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a support ticket">
    If you have the level role but your balance was not updated, open a support ticket in the server. Staff will be able to verify your level, check the reward logs, and manually issue the missing Pokécoins if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Staff **can** assist with missing level-up rewards — this is a known edge case. Just open a ticket with your username and the level you reached, and the team will sort it out.
</Note>

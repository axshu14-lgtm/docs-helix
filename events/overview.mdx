Events are daily activities hosted in the Helixia server. Participating in any one event per day completes your **"Participate in 1 Daily Event"** quest, earning you **30,000 PC**.

There are three types of events:

  <Card icon="mug-hot" href="/events/tea-event" title="Tea Event">
    Word-based game hosted by the Mudae bot.

  <Card icon="grid" href="/events/bingo-event" title="Bingo Event">

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Quest Credit

You only need to participate in **one** event per day to complete the daily event quest. You don't need to win — simply participating is enough.

If you participate in multiple events in the same day, the quest is marked complete after the first one. There's no extra reward for additional events beyond the fun of playing.

## Event Schedule

| Event       | Time (IST)               | Frequency   |
| ----------- | ------------------------ | ----------- |
| Roll Event  | 5:00 PM and 4:00 AM      | Twice daily |
| Tea Event   | Varies (staff-initiated) | Daily       |
| Bingo Event | Varies (staff-initiated) | Daily       |

<Note>
  Staff members receive reminders at **5:30 PM IST** for the Tea event and **8:00 PM IST** for the Bingo event, so these typically happen around those times. The exact schedule may vary.
</Note>

## How Quest Credit Is Awarded

For all three event types, quest credit is awarded **after the event ends**, not instantly when you join. This ensures only actual participants receive credit.

- **Roll Event** — credit goes to everyone who used `.roll` during the event
- **Tea Event** — credit goes to everyone who reacted within the first 15 seconds
- **Bingo Event** — credit goes to everyone mentioned as a participant by the Sugari bot
